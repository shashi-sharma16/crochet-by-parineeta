ABOUT

Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm Parineeta Khaire, the creator behind Parineeta's Crochet, where every stitch is made with love, patience, and creativity. What started as a simple hobby soon became a passion for creating handmade crochet pieces that spread happiness and bring smiles to people's faces...

Over the past six months, I've enjoyed exploring new ideas, experimenting with unique designs, and turning colourful yarn into meaningful keepsakes. Every bouquet, flower, keychain, and custom creation is handcrafted by me with care and attention to every detail.

I believe handmade gifts hold a special warmth, creating memories that last far beyond the moment they're received. Whether you're choosing something from my collection or requesting a custom piece, my goal is to create something cozy, thoughtful, elegant, and truly one of a kind.

Thank you for supporting my small business and being a part of this journey. 💗

Before You Order

Here are a few things you should know before placing your order.

  • DM us a screenshot of the product you'd like to order or share your custom idea.
  • Choose your preferred colors and customizations.
  • We'll confirm the price and availability.
  • An advance payment is required to confirm your order.
  • Shipping charges may apply depending on your location.
  • For shipping details, simply DM us "Shipping Charges" on Instagram.
  • Processing time is approximately 1–3 weeks.
  • Every crochet item is handmade with care.
  • Urgent orders may not always be possible.
  • Mumbai: 2–3 business days after dispatch.
  • Outside Mumbai: 5–7 business days after dispatch.
  • We accept UPI payments.
  • Full payment or the required advance payment is needed to confirm your order.
  • Your order will begin only after payment confirmation.
  • Colors and designs can be customized.
  • Reference pictures are always welcome.
  • Every handmade piece may have slight variations, making it unique.
  • Handle your crochet creations gently and keep them away from water.
  • Some designs may not be possible to recreate.
  • Availability depends on yarn, materials, and pattern limitations.
  • We'll always let you know honestly if a design cannot be made.
  • Please be kind and patient, as every item is handcrafted with care.
